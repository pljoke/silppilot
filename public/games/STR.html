How to Play

This test has three phases:

  • Phase 1: Memorize - You will have 3 minutes to read and memorize a short story. In Practice Mode, you can proceed whenever you are ready.
  • Phase 2: Math Problems - You will be given 3 minutes to solve 12 math problems. This is to distract your short-term memory.
  • Phase 3: Recall - You will have 3 minutes to answer 15 questions about the story you memorized.

Good luck!
